Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

NotiSnap is designed to help users archive and recover notifications received on their Android device while keeping their data private.

Information Stored

NotiSnap stores notification content received by your device in order to provide notification history and recovery features.

Local Storage

All notification records remain stored locally on your device. NotiSnap does not upload, synchronize, or transmit notification data to external servers.

Internet Usage

NotiSnap does not require internet access for its core functionality. Notification history is archived and managed locally on your device.

Data Sharing

NotiSnap does not collect personal information, sell user data, or share notification content with third parties.

Permissions

NotiSnap requires Notification Access permission in order to archive notifications received by your device.

Retention and Deletion

Notification records may be automatically removed according to user-configured retention periods and archive limits. Users may also manually delete notification cards from within the app.

Backups

NotiSnap does not currently provide cloud backup, synchronization, or export functionality. If notification records are removed or the application is uninstalled, those records cannot be recovered through NotiSnap.
